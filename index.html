About Me

Hello!

My name is Preston, and I'm a data scientist based out of Washington State. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

Working in the healthcare industry, I specialize in the analyzing and visualizing of data using a variety of different tools. These include Microsoft Excel, Python, R, SQL, STATA, amongst others.
