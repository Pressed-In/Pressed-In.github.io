ABOUT ME

Hello!

My name is Preston, and I'm a data analyst based out of Washington State. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

Currently working in the healthcare industry, I specialize in analyzing, visualizing, and communicating data, using a variety of different tools and methods. These tools include Microsoft Excel, Python, R, SQL, and STATA, amongst others. I even coded this website!
